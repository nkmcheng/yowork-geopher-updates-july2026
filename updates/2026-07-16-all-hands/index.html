Mobile Responsiveness

YoWork now adapts to your phone — check tasks, approve, and sign without waiting to be back at a desk.

  • 1
    The core screens are reworked for small screens — navigation, boards, and forms now fit a phone instead of forcing a desktop layout.
  • 2
    E-signature works on mobile — review a document and sign it right from your phone when a request comes in.
  • 3
    No app to install — it's the same YoWork URL, opened in your phone's browser.

YoWork · Section 2

Daily Snapshot

The daily snapshot now lives inside YoWork — one native flow instead of separate Lark tables per team.

  • 1
    Submit your daily snapshot directly in YoWork — no more hunting for your team's Lark table.
  • 2
    It gathers your day's activity for you — Lark chats and updates, meetings on your calendar, and your YoWork activity — so the snapshot reflects what you actually did.
  • 3
    Snapshots land in one place per team, making the day's progress easy for leads to review.
Native in YoWorkLark chats & updatesCalendar meetingsYoWork activity
YoWork · Section 3

Daily Snapshot Settings

Under Settings → Scheduling — let YoWork draft your snapshot on a schedule, so you just review & approve.

  • 1
    Turn on Generate Daily Snapshot and it auto-drafts your snapshot from today's activity at the time you set.
  • 2
    Pick the time, timezone, and where you're notified — in-app, Lark, or both — or hit Run Now anytime.
  • 3
    When it runs, you get a nudge: "Your daily snapshot is ready" — open it, review, and approve.
