E-Signature

The native e-signature platform already centralizes approvals — PDF field placement, multi-signer flows, delegation, full audit trails. Three updates make it faster and more open:

Sign All

E-Signature

Sign All

One action completes every field assigned to you — no more clicking through them one at a time.

  • 1
    A single "Sign All" action fills every pending field assigned to you across the whole document.
  • 2
    Removes the field-by-field clicking that slowed down documents with signatures or initials on every page.
  • 3
    Each field still records its own audit log entry — the speed-up costs you nothing in traceability.
Faster signingSame audit trail
E-Signature

Bulk Sign Requests

Send the same document to many recipients in one go, instead of creating each request by hand.

  • 1
    Create and send many signature requests at once from a single source document.
  • 2
    Built for high-volume, repeatable flows — HR onboarding packets, vendor NDAs, policy acknowledgements.
  • 3
    Every recipient still gets their own tracked request, notification, and audit history.
High-volume readyPer-recipient tracking
E-Signature

External Signing Links

For when the counterparty asks us to sign on their own platform — think DocuSign — instead of sending a PDF.

  • 1
    Attach the third-party signing link (e.g. DocuSign) to a request instead of downloading, signing and re-uploading a PDF.
  • 2
    YoWork still tracks the request end-to-end, even though signing happens on the external platform.
  • 3
    Signers get Lark notifications and follow-up reminders until it's completed — no manual chasing.
Third-party linksNo download / re-uploadLark notifications

Geopher · Payments

Xendit Payments

Purchase a plan — or switch between plans — right through Xendit.

  • 1
    Buy a plan or upgrade / downgrade directly through Xendit's checkout.
  • 2
    Checkout is hosted by Xendit, so card details never touch Geopher's servers.
  • 3
    Subscriptions are created and tracked on Xendit — plan changes apply to the account right away.
  • 4
    A built-in manage & cancel flow lets customers change or end their plan in-app.
Hosted checkoutUpgrade / downgradePCI-friendly
Geopher · Brand Analytics

Brand Relation Tagging & Dedup

Cleaner brand data means a more trustworthy competitive analysis.

  • 1
    Automatically detects and groups name variations — "Acme", "Acme Corp", "@acme" — into one alias group.
  • 2
    All the mentions from those variations are counted together, so a brand's real total isn't split across separate names.
  • 3
    Users can accept or reject suggested aliases, and ignore brands that shouldn't be tracked at all.
  • 4
    Cleaner, de-duplicated data feeds straight into brand ranking and the competitive leaderboard.
Alias detectionIgnore listCleaner rankings
